The Top-5 Rated movies

According to IMBD

1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful drama about hope, friendship, and endurance within a corrupt prison system.

2. The Godfather (1972)

The legendary cinematic masterpiece chronicling the Corleone crime family.

3. The Dark Knight (2008)

Widely considered the greatest superhero film, pitting Batman against the Joker.

4. The Godfather Part II (1974)

The brilliant sequel that continues the Corleone saga while flashing back to a young Vito Corleone.

5. 12 Angry Men (1957)

A gripping, intense courtroom drama that takes place almost entirely in a single jury room.
